Stay informed and keep your users engaged with automatic email notifications whenever a new product is created on your WordPress website.


== Description ==
  The Product Announcer plugin for WordPress keeps your users informed by sending them automatic email notifications whenever a new product is created on your website. This ensures that your users never miss out on important updates and new offerings. With customizable email templates and easy-to-use settings, you can tailor notifications to suit your audience's preferences and enhance user engagement. The plugin is SEO-friendly and seamlessly integrates with the WordPress dashboard.

 Features:
- ** Automatic email notifications for new product creations.
- ** Easy-to-use settings for personalized notifications.
- ** Enhance user engagement and retention.
- ** Customizable email templates to match your brand's style.
- ** Customizable email templates to suit audience preferences.
- ** User-friendly settings for easy configuration.
- ** Enhances user engagement and keeps users informed.
- ** SEO-friendly implementation for better visibility.
- ** Seamless integration with the WordPress dashboard.
- And much more!

== Installation ==
 1. Upload the `product-announcer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
 2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
 3. Navigate to the plugin settings in your WordPress dashboard to customize email templates and notification settings.
